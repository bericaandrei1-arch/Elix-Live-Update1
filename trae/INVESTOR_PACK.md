## 2. Financial Metrics (The "Standard 6")

### Revenue & Growth
*   **MRR / ARR**:
    *   *Calculation*: (VIP Subscriptions) + (Avg Monthly Coin Spend * 0.8).
    *   *Target*: 20% MoM growth.
*   **Gross Margin**:
    *   *Calculation*: (Total Revenue - Creator Payouts - Hosting/Stripe Fees) / Total Revenue.
    *   *Target*: > 40% (Marketplace Standard).

### Unit Economics
*   **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**:
    *   *Calculation*: Total Marketing Spend / New Paying Users.
    *   *Target*: Payback < 6 months.
*   **LTV (Lifetime Value)**:
    *   *Calculation*: ARPU * (1 / Churn Rate).
    *   *Ratio*: LTV:CAC > 3:1.
*   **Churn**:
    *   *Calculation*: % of Paying Users (Month N) who didn't pay in Month N+1.

### Health
*   **Burn Rate**:
    *   *Calculation*: Monthly Operating Expenses - Monthly Gross Profit.
*   **Runway**:
    *   *Calculation*: Cash in Bank / Monthly Burn Rate.
    *   *Target*: > 18 months.
