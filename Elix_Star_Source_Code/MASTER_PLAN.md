# Growth & Monetization Master Plan
**System**: Elix Star Live Good MVP
**Status**: Investor Ready / Growth Phase

This master plan governs the revenue generation and expansion of the company.

---

## 1. Revenue Model
**Goal**: Diversified, recurring revenue.

*   **Primary**: Coin Purchases (Micro-transactions).
*   **Secondary**: Creator Revenue Share (50% Take Rate).
*   **Tertiary**: VIP Subscriptions (Planned).
*   **Reference**: [Monetization System](./MONETIZATION.md)

---

## 2. Growth Roadmap
**Goal**: Viral Acquisition + Geographic Expansion.

*   **Acquisition**: TikTok Viral Clips + Referral System.
*   **Virality**: Share-to-unlock, Invite Rewards.
*   **Retention**: Leveling System, Seasonal Events.
*   **Reference**: [Growth Strategy](./GROWTH_STRATEGY.md)

---

## 3. Expansion Plan
**Goal**: Global Domination in 3 Phases.

1.  **Phase 1 (Now)**: UK & EU. Focus on English content + GDPR.
2.  **Phase 2**: USA. Trigger at 50k MAU.
3.  **Phase 3**: MENA & Asia. Localization required.
*   **Reference**: [Expansion Strategy](./GROWTH_STRATEGY.md#3-expansion-strategy-roadmap)

---

## 4. Investor Metrics
**Goal**: "The Standard 6" for Series A.

1.  **MRR**: 20% MoM Growth.
2.  **CAC**: Payback < 6 months.
3.  **LTV**: > 3x CAC.
4.  **Gross Margin**: > 40%.
5.  **Burn Rate**: Managed to 18mo runway.
6.  **Churn**: < 5% Monthly.
*   **Reference**: [Investor Pack](./INVESTOR_PACK.md)

---

## 5. Compliance Status
**Goal**: Zero Regulatory Risk.

*   **GDPR**: Compliant (Data Rights defined).
*   **KYC/AML**: Stripe Identity integration required for payouts.
*   **Age**: 18+ strict enforcement for streaming.
*   **Reference**: [Compliance & Legal](./COMPLIANCE_LEGAL.md)

## 6. Risk Register
**Goal**: Mitigate existential threats.

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
